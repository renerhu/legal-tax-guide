About Tax & Legal Guide

Last updated: May 2026

Tax & Legal Guide was created to make US tax and legal information accessible to everyone — freelancers, entrepreneurs, and small business owners who don't have the budget for expensive attorneys or CPAs.

What We Cover

  • Tax Guides — Step-by-step instructions for filing taxes, maximizing deductions, and staying compliant
  • LLC Formation — Everything about setting up and maintaining a Limited Liability Company
  • IRS & Compliance — Understanding IRS rules, forms, audits, and how to stay on the right side of the law
  • Small Business Law — Contracts, liability, intellectual property, and other legal essentials
  • Money-Saving Strategies — Legal ways to reduce your tax burden and optimize your finances
